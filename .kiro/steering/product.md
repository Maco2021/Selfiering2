# Product: SelfieRing

SelfieRing is a single-page marketing website for a photo booth / selfie ring rental service operating in Moldova (Romanian-speaking market). The site showcases the service to potential clients for events such as weddings, parties, and corporate gatherings.

## Key Sections
- **Intro** — Hero with animated logo and rotating product images
- **Photo** — Card-stack photo gallery of sample shots
- **Video** — Video showcase with thumbnail selector
- **Album** — Auto-scrolling image strip
- **Reviews (Recenzii)** — Customer testimonials with photos
- **Clients (Hexagon)** — Partner/client logos in a honeycomb layout
- **Contact (Contacte)** — Contact form powered by EmailJS

## Target Audience
Romanian-speaking customers in Moldova looking to rent a selfie ring / photo booth for events.

## Language
UI copy is in Romanian. Code comments may appear in Russian or English.
